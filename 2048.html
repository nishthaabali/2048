HOW TO PLAY "2048" is a puzzle game played on a 4x4 grid where you combine matching tiles by sliding them in four directions. Your goal is to create a tile with the value 2048. Plan your moves strategically, as the grid fills up quickly, and the game ends when there are no more valid moves. "2048" is a fun and challenging game that tests your logical thinking and puzzle-solving skills.
